Induction Program & Classes Schedule for Newly Admitted Students

Academic Year 2025-26
Subject: Induction Program and Class Commencement for newly admitted B.Tech Computer Science & Business Systems (CSBS) and B.Des First Year Students

This is to inform all newly admitted students of B.Tech Computer Science & Business Systems (CSBS) and B.Des programs for the academic year 2025-26 that the admission process has been successfully completed.

IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENTS:

1. INDUCTION PROGRAM

  • The Induction Program for all newly admitted B.Tech and B.Des first year students has been RESCHEDULED.
  • New Dates: 28th August 2025 to 29th August 2025
  • Detailed induction program schedule will be notified separately.
  • The program will cover orientation, academic guidelines, and campus familiarization.

2. REGULAR CLASSES

  • Regular academic classes will commence from 18th August 2025.
  • All newly admitted first year students MUST report at IET campus on 18th August 2025.
  • Attendance is mandatory from the first day of classes.

3. CLASS TIMETABLE & ACADEMIC INFORMATION

  • Detailed class timetable will be published on the official IET website.
  • Course syllabus and academic calendar will be distributed during orientation.
  • Students are advised to regularly check the website for updates.

4. REPORTING INSTRUCTIONS

  • All admitted students must report to the college office on 18th August 2025.
  • Bring all original documents and admission-related papers.
  • Complete any pending documentation or fee payment procedures.
  • Collect student ID cards and academic materials.

IMPORTANT NOTE:

All students must regularly monitor the official IET DAVV website (www.ietdavv.edu.in) for any further updates, notices, or changes in the schedule. Failure to report on time may result in cancellation of admission.
